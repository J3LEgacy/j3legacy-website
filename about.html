The Story

Why I Started J3LEgacy

It started with a moment I couldn't ignore. I was at a big-box electronics store, waiting to ask about a screen repair, when I watched an older couple approach the service counter with their laptop. They just wanted help.

What they got instead was a $100 diagnostic fee estimate — just to look at it — followed immediately by a push to buy antivirus software they almost certainly didn't need. I've worked in technology long enough to know those upsells are rarely necessary. And I could see on their faces that they felt confused, pressured, and a little embarrassed.

I wanted to step in and help them right then and there. And that moment crystallized something I'd been thinking about for a long time: there is a real gap in the market for honest, patient, transparent tech support — especially for people who don't feel comfortable with technology.

Combined with values I learned in the military — accountability, mission focus, taking care of the people beside you — J3LEgacy Technology Solutions was born. We are not Geek Squad. We are not a franchise. We are a local, veteran-owned business that shows up with integrity every single time.
