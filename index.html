Why We Started

The Moment That Started Everything

While waiting at a big-box electronics store, I watched an older couple ask for help with their laptop. They were immediately told they'd need to pay nearly $100 just for a diagnostic — and before they could blink, the conversation shifted to selling them antivirus software they almost certainly didn't need.

I knew from experience that most of those upsells are unnecessary — and that what those folks actually needed was someone who would sit down with them, explain what was really going on, and fix it fairly.

That moment, combined with my military background, reinforced everything I believe about service: you show up with integrity, you put the person first, and you don't take advantage of people who trust you.

"We don't hack networks — we help people understand and protect them. Honest tech help, without the upsell. That's the whole mission."

— J3LEgacy Technology Solutions
