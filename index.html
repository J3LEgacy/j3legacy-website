Our Story

Why J3Legacy Exists

It Started With a Moment That Changed Everything

I was standing in the Geek Squad line at Best Buy, watching an elderly couple get talked into a $300+ "protection plan" they didn't need — on top of the overpriced repair. The confusion on their faces. The way they just nodded along because they didn't want to seem difficult.

That moment stuck with me. As a military veteran, I was trained to lead with integrity. To never take advantage of the people you serve. I saw an industry that had forgotten that — and I knew I could do better.

J3Legacy Technology Solutions was built on a simple promise: honest, plain-English tech help without the upsell. We started helping homeowners and seniors with computer repairs, and we've grown into a full managed IT provider for small businesses across Orange County.

Whether you're a five-person law firm or a dental practice with twenty workstations, we treat your business like it's our own. Because in our community, your success is our legacy.
